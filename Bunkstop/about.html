Do you want to know more about us?

RAJAT CHOUDHARY

Bunkstop exists because there is a need for clean, hygienic, air-conditioned, affordable accommodation in our country. We need to show the world that India is adapting to a need that is very much there.

We were tired of listening to things like: "Everything is so expensive today and only if you spend big money is when you can derive quality." We as a chain of hostels would like to change that perception, fulfill a need that exists in our country, to help people coming from foreign countries feel at home. We believe in the phrase .

Athithi devo bhava Geeta

which means that "The guest is equivalent to God

At Bunkstop our philosophy is that "All good things are not necessarily expensive." Thus, we provide quality at affordable prices.
